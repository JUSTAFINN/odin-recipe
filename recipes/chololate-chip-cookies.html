Ingredients

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then add eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolbe baking soda in hot water and add it to the mixture.
  3. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Drop Dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake at 350 f (180 c) for about 10 minutes until the edges are golden brown.
  6. Remove from oven, allow to cool.
  7. Enjoy!
